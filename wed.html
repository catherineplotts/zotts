Their Love Story


Catherine and Mike met and fell in love in high school. They even attended Ocean Township's senior prom together! In 2013, they moved to Rhode Island together to start their adult lives. They have been happily residing in Providence ever since. In October 2015, Mike popped the question. They were on the last stop of their New England Fall Foliage vacation -- a pumpkin patch -- when Mike got down on one knee and asked Catherine to marry him. Of course, Catherine said yes. The wedding will be on September 9th, 2016 in Providence. The couple hopes you can attend!
